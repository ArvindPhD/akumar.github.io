About

I am an Associate Professor at the Department of Computational Sciences, with a research focus on artificial intelligence applications in biology, genomics, and complex data systems.

My work bridges biology, statistics, and computer science, aiming to develop interpretable models that improve our understanding of biological processes.

I have published over 50 peer-reviewed papers in leading journals such as *Nature Computational Science* and *Bioinformatics*.

Research Interests

Artificial Intelligence

Development of interpretable AI models for biomedical data and predictive analytics.

Computational Biology

Modeling complex molecular interactions using network-based and statistical methods.

Data Science

Integrating high-dimensional datasets for actionable insights in healthcare research.
